Roommates

Privacy Policy for Roommates

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Roommates ("the app") is a shared chore and expense tracker for household members. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. This is used to identify you within your household group.

Household Data: Chore assignments, completion history, expense records, and balances you enter are stored in the cloud so your roommates can see them. This is core to how the app works.

Third-Party Services — Firebase

The app uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data. You can review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

Your household data is stored in Google Firestore (cloud). Deleting the app does not delete your account or household data. To delete your data, contact us at the email below.

Data Sharing

Your chore and expense data is visible to members of your household group. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties outside of Firebase as described above.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Find my Car

Privacy Policy for Find My Car

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Find My Car ("the app") is a simple parking location saver. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data We Collect

Location: The app accesses your device's GPS location only when you tap "I Parked Here." Your location is saved locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to our servers because we don't have any.

Photos: If you choose to add a photo of your parking spot, that photo is stored locally on your device. It is never uploaded or shared.

Notes: Any text notes you add are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

On iOS, you will be asked for permission to track before personalized ads are shown. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Data Storage

All your parking data (location, photos, notes) is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. Deleting the app removes all data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with anyone. No data leaves your device except through Google AdMob's ad network as described above.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Support

For support or questions, contact: abbysfunbuilds@gmail.com

Privacy Policy for Zone: White Noise & Focus

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Zone: White Noise & Focus ("the app") is an ambient noise and focus sound app. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data We Collect

Purchase Status: If you purchase Zone Pro, the app stores a record of your purchase status locally on your device so the app remembers your unlock. This data is never transmitted to our servers because we don't have any. Purchase verification is handled entirely by Apple.

App Preferences: Your sound preferences, custom mixes, and timer settings are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded or shared.

Data Storage

All app data (preferences, custom sounds, purchase status) is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, trade, or share any personal data. No data leaves your device. The app does not connect to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
